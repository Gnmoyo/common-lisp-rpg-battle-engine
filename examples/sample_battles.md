# Sample Battle Scenarios

## Example Behaviors

### Ghost vs Non-Magical Weapon
A ghost cannot be damaged by a normal weapon.

### Ghost vs Magical Weapon
A magical weapon can damage a ghost successfully.

### Zombie Regeneration
A zombie regenerates health after being hit, making it harder to defeat.

### Ogre Transformation
An ogre transforms into a ghost when defeated, creating multi-stage enemy behavior.

### Holy Weapon vs Undead
Holy weapons deal increased damage to undead enemies.

## Why these examples matter

These examples demonstrate:
- polymorphism
- rule-based behavior
- extensible object-oriented design
- complex interactions between entity and weapon types
